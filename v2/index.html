FAQ

Common Questions

Do you need access to my LinkedIn account?
No. We never ask for your LinkedIn login. Our system analyzes publicly available engagement data. No bots, no scraping of your account, no risk to your profile.
Do you send messages on my behalf?
Never. We provide intelligence and pre-written intro request emails. You decide who to reach out to, when, and how. Every action is 100% in your control.
What's a Center of Influence?
A Center of Influence (COI) is someone who already trusts you and has connections to people who could become your clients. Think: current clients, referral partners, mentors, strategic partners. People who would gladly make an introduction if asked the right way.
How are prospects scored?
Each prospect is scored across 5 dimensions: ICP fit (do they match your ideal customer profile), warmth (strength of their relationship with your COI), authority (are they a decision-maker), activity (are they active on LinkedIn), and strategic value (would they open doors to other opportunities).
How fast do I see results?
You'll receive your first intelligence report within 24 hours of onboarding. Most clients have their first intro request sent within the first week. We analyze a new Center of Influence every weekday.
Is this just for B2B?
Yes. The Inroad Engine is designed for B2B businesses, professional services, and anyone whose revenue is driven by relationships and referrals. If you sell to other businesses or professionals, this is for you.
