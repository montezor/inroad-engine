FAQ

Common Questions

Is this a LinkedIn Sales Navigator alternative?
Yes and no. Sales Navigator is built for cold outreach via InMail. Inroad Engine is built for warm introductions through people who already trust you. They solve different problems. But if your goal is warm referrals, our system is purpose-built for exactly that.
Can I use both tools together?
Absolutely. Some teams use Sales Navigator for broad prospecting and the Inroad Engine specifically for warm introduction paths. They complement each other well.
Do you need access to my LinkedIn account?
No. We never ask for your LinkedIn login. Our system analyzes publicly available engagement data. No bots, no scraping of your account, no risk to your profile.
How much does Sales Navigator cost vs your system?
Sales Navigator costs $99–$179/month per user and still requires you to do the manual research on who's warm. The Inroad Engine is a done-for-you intelligence service that delivers scored, prioritized warm intro opportunities directly to you.
What does the Inroad Engine do that Sales Navigator can't?
Sales Navigator shows you who's connected to whom. We show you who's actively engaged with your Centers of Influence, score them against your ICP, rank them by warmth, and write the intro request emails for you. It's the difference between a phone book and a personal concierge.
