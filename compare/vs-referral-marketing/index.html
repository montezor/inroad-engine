FAQ

Common Questions

How is this different from a referral program?
Traditional referral programs are passive. You wait and hope people think of you. The Inroad Engine is proactive. We use AI to analyze your existing relationships, identify the warmest paths to ideal prospects, and give you the exact ask to make. You don't wait for referrals. You engineer them.
Do you use referral incentives or rewards?
No. Incentivized referrals feel transactional and often produce lower-quality introductions. The Inroad Engine focuses on warm introductions that feel natural. One professional helping another. No gift cards, no kickbacks. Just genuine relationship leverage.
How many referrals can I expect?
Most traditional referral programs produce 2-5 referrals per year. Our system typically generates 20-40+ intro requests per quarter by systematically analyzing your Centers of Influence every business day.
What if my network is small?
You don't need a massive network. Even 10-20 strong Centers of Influence can produce hundreds of warm introduction opportunities. The system finds the hidden value in your existing relationships. You'd be surprised how many warm paths exist that you've never seen.
Is this a platform or a service?
It's a done-for-you intelligence service. We analyze your network, score prospects, identify warm introduction paths, and write the outreach for you. You're not logging into a dashboard hoping for referrals. You're receiving actionable intelligence delivered to your inbox.
