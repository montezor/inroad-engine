FAQ

Common Questions

What is a warm introduction system?
A warm introduction system uses AI to analyze your existing relationships and identify people in your network who can introduce you to ideal prospects. Instead of cold emailing strangers, you get introduced through trusted mutual connections.
Is cold email dead?
Not dead, but dramatically less effective. With tighter spam filters, GDPR/CAN-SPAM regulations, and general buyer fatigue, cold email response rates have dropped to 2-5%. Meanwhile, warm introductions consistently achieve 40-60% meeting rates.
How much does cold email cost compared to warm introductions?
Cold email infrastructure (tools, domains, data providers, SDR salaries) typically costs $3,000-$10,000+/month. When you factor in the low conversion rate, cost per meeting is often $500-$2,000+. Warm introductions through the Inroad Engine produce meetings at a fraction of that cost.
Do warm introductions really close faster?
Yes. Research consistently shows referred prospects close 4-5x faster than cold prospects. They already have built-in trust from the mutual connection, so the sales cycle is dramatically shorter.
Can I replace my entire cold email operation?
Many teams do. However, some companies use both. Cold email for volume at the top of funnel, and warm introductions for high-value target accounts. The Inroad Engine is most effective as your primary pipeline channel for quality deals.
