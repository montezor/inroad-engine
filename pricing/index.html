Pricing FAQ

Common questions about plans, billing, and getting started.

How long does onboarding take?
Most clients are fully onboarded within 48 hours. We'll map your initial Centers of Influence, configure your dashboard, and deliver your first intelligence report within the first week. Growth and Enterprise plans include a guided onboarding call.
Are there any long-term contracts?
No. All plans are month-to-month. You can cancel anytime from your dashboard with no penalties, no fees, and no hoops to jump through. We earn your business every month.
What's your refund policy?
We offer a full 30-day money-back guarantee on all plans. If the Inroad Engine doesn't surface valuable introduction opportunities in your first month, we'll refund 100% of your payment. No questions asked.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan later?
Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. When you upgrade, you'll get immediate access to additional features. Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
What CRMs do you integrate with?
The Growth plan includes native integrations with HubSpot, Salesforce, and Pipedrive. Enterprise plans support custom CRM integrations. We're adding new integrations regularly based on client demand.
What exactly is a "Center of Influence"?
A Center of Influence (COI) is a person in your professional network who has connections to your ideal clients. Our AI maps these individuals, tracks their activity and relationships, and alerts you when warm introduction opportunities arise. Think of it as having a 24/7 research assistant watching your network for referral signals.
Do you offer annual billing?
Yes. Contact our sales team during your demo to discuss annual billing options. Annual plans include a meaningful discount and priority feature access.
Is my data secure?
Your data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We follow SOC 2 security practices and never share your network data with third parties. Your competitive intelligence stays yours.
