Partner FAQ

Common Questions

How much commission do I earn?▾
Partners earn 25% recurring commission on every member who signs up through their referral. That's $75/month per member at the standard $300/month price point. This isn't a one-time bonus. You earn every single month, as long as the member stays active.
Is there a cost to become a partner?▾
No. Zero cost to join. Zero risk. We don't charge partners anything. You recommend the tool, and if members sign up, you earn. Simple.
Do I need to provide support to my members?▾
Not at all. We handle 100% of onboarding, training, support, and delivery. Your only job is making the introduction. We take it from there.
Can you present to my group directly?▾
Absolutely. We're happy to do a live presentation or webinar for your group. We'll show the tool, walk through the value, and answer questions. Most groups love the demo because they immediately see how it applies to their business.
Is there a white-label option?▾
Yes. For enterprise partners or larger organizations, we offer white-label options where the system can be branded under your organization's name. Get in touch and we'll discuss what makes sense for your use case.
How do I track my referrals and earnings?▾
Every partner gets access to a dashboard where you can see who's signed up, your monthly earnings, and payout history. Full transparency, no guesswork.
What kind of businesses benefit most?▾
Any B2B professional who relies on relationships and referrals. Think: financial advisors, insurance professionals, attorneys, consultants, commercial real estate agents, accountants, marketing agencies, IT services. If they're in a networking group, this tool is built for them.
How soon can I start earning?▾
As soon as your first member signs up. The approval process takes about one business day, and from there you can start sharing immediately. Some partners see their first sign-ups within the first week.
