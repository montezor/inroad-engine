The Reality

Financial Services Is Built on Referrals. You Already Know This.

Wealth advisors refer to CPAs. CPAs refer to estate attorneys. Insurance brokers refer to financial planners. The entire industry is a web of Centers of Influence. The problem is that most of those COI relationships are invisible, sitting in personal networks nobody has mapped.

🕸️

COI Networks Are Invisible

Your team has hundreds of relationships with CPAs, attorneys, and advisors. But nobody knows who knows who. Those referral paths are locked in individual Rolodexes and LinkedIn profiles.

🚫

Cold Outreach Is a Compliance Headache

Financial services is one of the most regulated industries. Cold emails and calls carry compliance risk. Warm introductions are not just more effective. They're safer.

💰

High-Value Deals Need Trust

Nobody hands over their financial life to someone who cold-called them. Wealth management, insurance, and advisory deals require deep trust that only comes from a credible referral.

Wealth Mgmt

Wealthminder

RIA and wealth advisor intelligence. Detailed firm profiles, AUM data, fee structures, and advisor biographies. Built for wholesalers and asset managers prospecting advisory firms.

Best for: Wholesalers and asset managers who use Wealthminder to target RIAs. Find warm paths through shared COI networks and industry associations.
Compliance Intel

BrokerCheck (FINRA)

FINRA's official broker and advisor lookup. Registration history, exam qualifications, employment history, and regulatory actions for every registered rep in the U.S.

Best for: Teams targeting specific brokers or advisors. Cross-reference registration data with your network to find who can make the introduction to the right rep at the right firm.
RIA Intel

RIA Database

Comprehensive registered investment advisor data. Form ADV filings, AUM, client demographics, custodian relationships, and investment strategies for thousands of RIA firms.

Best for: Asset managers, custodians, and fintech vendors targeting RIAs by AUM tier, investment style, or custodial platform. Map warm paths to the principals.
Market Intel

Vertical IQ

Industry intelligence for financial professionals. Detailed industry profiles, economic trends, and call prep reports designed to help financial advisors win new business.

Best for: Financial institutions and fintechs using Vertical IQ for market research. Pair industry intelligence with warm introductions to target the right advisors in the right verticals.
