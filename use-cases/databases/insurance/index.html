Supported Databases

Works With Every Major Insurance Database

Export your prospect list from any of these platforms. We handle the rest.

FINRA

BrokerCheck (FINRA)

Broker and advisor registration records. Search by firm, license type, location, and disciplinary history. The official FINRA database used to verify and research financial professionals across the country.

Best for: Insurance agents targeting financial advisors for cross-referral partnerships. Upload your advisor target list and find who on your team already knows them.
Ratings

AM Best

Insurance company ratings and financial data. The industry standard for evaluating carrier financial strength, operating performance, and market position. Used by agents, brokers, and regulators industry-wide.

Best for: Agencies targeting specific carriers or MGAs for appointments. Find warm paths to the underwriters and regional managers who control appointments.
Regulatory

NAIC Database

State insurance department regulatory filings. Comprehensive data on licensed entities, market conduct, financial filings, and regulatory actions across all 50 states and territories.

Best for: Compliance teams and agencies building target lists by state and line of business. Cross-reference regulatory data with your network for warm introductions.
Directory

InsuranceJobs / Industry Directories

Agent and agency directories organized by state, specialty, and line of business. Professional directories that list producers, agencies, wholesalers, and MGAs across every insurance vertical.

Best for: Wholesalers and MGAs targeting retail agents by geography and line. Upload your directory export and discover which agents your team already has relationships with.
